# Unlocking Sanskrit: Crafting a Tokenizer for the Language of the Gods
<span style="color: #00d4ff; display: inline;">This introductory post outlines the objectives of my project, setting the stage for what's to come. Stay tuned for further updates, including technical details and documentation, as I progress!</span>

In this project, I am developing a sophisticated tokenizer tailored for processing Sanskrit texts in Large Language Models (LLMs). Recognizing Sanskrit's unique structural and grammatical complexities, my aim is to create a tokenizer that accurately segments Sanskrit scripts, enhancing LLMs' understanding and generation capabilities for this ancient language. This initiative aims to bridge a significant gap in NLP research, contributing to the broader inclusivity and accessibility of AI technologies across diverse linguistic traditions.
